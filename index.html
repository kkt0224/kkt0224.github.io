About Me

I am currently a Researcher at the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI) in South Korea. I received my B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Kyung Hee University. Before joining ETRI, I served as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Kyung Hee University and expanded my research horizons as a Visiting Researcher at the Bradley Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech. My research primarily focuses on AI-based networks, encompassing both wired and wireless communication systems. I am highly proficient in applying mathematical optimization and AI-based solutions to tackle complex networking and system challenges.

Research Interests

  • AI-based Networks: Intelligent resource management and protocol design for wired and wireless networks.
  • Mathematical Optimization: Formulating and solving complex network problems using robust optimization techniques.
  • AI-based Solutions: Developing advanced machine learning and deep learning models for practical system implementations.
